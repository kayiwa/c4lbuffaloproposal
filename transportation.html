Your Stay

How to get here, where to stay

Transportation

Buffalo boasts of numerous alternative transportation options, including ride sharing, taxis, Reddy Bikeshare, and the Niagara Frontier Transportation Authority (NFTA) Metro Bus & Rail.

Airport to Hotel

UB's North Campus is just 15 minutes away from the Buffalo Niagara International Airport. Ground transportation is available to get you from the airport to your hotel. 

Campus Parking and Shuttles

University at Buffalo's fleet of buses and shuttles, known as the Stampede, is available to get around and between campuses quickly.

Downtown Buffalo

NFTA's Metro Rail provides a direct line from UB's South Campus to downtown Canalside. From metro stops, it's just a quick walk to many of Buffalo's entertainment and dining.
